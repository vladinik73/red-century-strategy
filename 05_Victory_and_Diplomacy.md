# Victory & Diplomacy

## Military Victory

Capture all capitals.

## Economic Victory

Control \>=75% of total integrated CityPower Hold for 5 turns (checked
end of round)

## Technological Victory

Research level 5 science 10 turn timer Captured integrated city resets
timer

## Diplomacy

-   Max 2 alliances
-   Min 6 turns before break
-   3 turn cooldown
