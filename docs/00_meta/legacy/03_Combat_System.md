# Combat System

## Damage Formula

Damage = BaseDamage + TerrainBonus + MoraleModifier - DefenseModifier

Minimum damage = 1

## Terrain

+1 attack from mountain -1 attacking mountain from plains

## Encirclement

4+ enemies around (8-directional) → -1 defense

## Units

-   Instant movement
-   Attack by clicking enemy
-   No attack/move buttons
-   No undo
